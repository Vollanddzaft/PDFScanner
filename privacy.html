Privacy Policy

Last updated: January 2026

This Privacy Policy explains how PDFScanner (“we”, “our”, or “the App”) collects, uses, and protects your information.

Information We Collect

The App does not collect or store personal data such as names, addresses, or contact information.

Scanned documents, images, and recognized text are processed locally on your device and are not uploaded to our servers.

Usage Data

We may collect anonymous usage data to improve app performance and user experience. This data does not identify you personally.

Subscriptions

Subscription purchases are processed by Apple through the App Store. We do not have access to your payment information. Apple may provide us with anonymous subscription status information.

Third-Party Services

The App uses Apple-provided services such as StoreKit. These services may collect information in accordance with Apple’s privacy policies.

Data Security

We take reasonable measures to protect your data. However, no method of transmission or storage is completely secure.

Children’s Privacy

The App is not intended for children under the age of 13. We do not knowingly collect personal data from children.

Changes to This Policy
